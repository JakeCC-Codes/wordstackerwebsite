# Word Stacker
> by JakeCCz

Word Stacker is an Online Time Capsule where users share important words/pharses which represent important memorial/historic events from history.

Contribute to the ever-growing stack of words, in Online Mode, or top the leaderboard, in Solo Mode.

The Online Word Tower is **publicly viewable** by passersby of the website.

### **Blockstack Content Guidelines**
- Blockstacks/Wordtowers will contain words/phrases entered by other users, where the most recent blocks are appended to the very top of the tower.
- Blocks display their content, usernametag and the time of date at which it was added.
- Blocks may be shared amongst other users using the link obtained during block interaction.
- Blocks are only ever added to the Blockstack, THEY **CANNOT BE MODIFIED NOR REMOVED**.

> If a block is ever removed from a given wordtower/blockstack, the tower will begin to crumble and completely destroyed at an instant.

### **Blockstack Content Rules**
1. Age-restricted or obscene content is **NOT ALLOWED**
2. Spamming is **NOT ALLOWED**, in Online Mode.
3. Advertising, Sharing of Links or Promtions of any kind are **NOT ALLOWED**

> ... in Online Mode _(in Solo Mode #dowhateveryoufeelidrclol)_

4. Conversations and Collabortory Events/Movements are allowed.
5. Content Creation is allowed.
6. Have Fun, while keeping responsible!
